# Module 03 — Exercises

## Exercise 1: Storage Report Trading
- [ ] For the next 4 Thursdays, write down the consensus estimate for the EIA gas storage report BEFORE the release
- [ ] Record the actual number, the deviation from consensus, and the 5-minute price move after release
- [ ] Is the price move proportional to the surprise? What other factors matter?

## Exercise 2: Seasonality Analysis
- [ ] Pull 5 years of Henry Hub front-month daily settlement prices
- [ ] Create a seasonal chart (overlay each year on a Jan-Dec x-axis)
- [ ] When does gas tend to be cheapest? Most expensive? How reliable is this pattern?

## Exercise 3: Storage vs. Price
- [ ] Plot weekly US working gas in storage (from EIA) alongside Henry Hub price for the last 3 years
- [ ] Also plot storage vs. 5-year average (the surplus/deficit)
- [ ] Which has a stronger relationship with price — absolute storage level or deviation from average?

## Exercise 4: Weather Model Impact
- [ ] Follow weather model runs for one week (GFS and ECMWF 6-10 day and 8-14 day forecasts)
- [ ] Note any significant changes (warm-to-cold or cold-to-warm shifts)
- [ ] Track how the gas market reacted to each model shift
- [ ] Write up: which model does the market trust more?

## Exercise 5: The March/April Widowmaker
- [ ] Pull 10 years of the March/April Henry Hub calendar spread
- [ ] Plot the spread value from 12 months before expiry to expiry for each year
- [ ] Identify the 2-3 most volatile years — what drove the move?
- [ ] What is the max gain and max loss on this spread over your dataset?

## Exercise 6: LNG Export Impact
- [ ] Find monthly US LNG export volumes (EIA data)
- [ ] Plot LNG exports vs. Henry Hub price
- [ ] When Freeport LNG went offline in June 2022, what happened to Henry Hub? Why?
- [ ] Calculate the current JKM-Henry Hub spread — is the LNG export margin positive?

## Exercise 7: Basis Deep Dive
- [ ] Pick 3 regional gas pricing points (e.g., SoCal Citygate, Chicago Citygate, Dominion South)
- [ ] Pull their basis differentials vs. Henry Hub for the past year
- [ ] For each, explain why the basis is positive or negative
- [ ] Identify one basis blowout event and explain the physical reason behind it
